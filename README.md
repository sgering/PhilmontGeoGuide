# Philmont Trek 12-15 Field Guide

Create a professionally designed, printable field guide for **Philmont Scout Ranch Trek 12-15 (66 miles)** that combines geology, natural history, archaeology, mining history, astronomy, photography, and Scouting into a single day-by-day companion.

Unlike a traditional hiking guide, this project explains **why the landscape looks the way it does**, helping hikers interpret mountains, valleys, rocks, forests, wildlife, and historic sites through the eyes of a geologist and naturalist. Each hiking day will include a customized route guide with maps, elevation profiles, geological interpretation, ecological observations, historical context, scenic viewpoints, and nighttime astronomy tailored to the trek itinerary.

The guide will synthesize information from USGS publications, New Mexico Bureau of Geology, peer-reviewed literature, Philmont resources, historic photographs, and trail maps. Modern geologic interpretations will be compared with classic references such as **USGS Professional Paper 505** to provide an accurate and engaging understanding of the region.

Each daily section will include:

* Scout Law theme and daily reflection
* Clone Wars inspirational quote
* Hike summary and statistics
* Route map and elevation profile
* Geology and landscape interpretation
* Mining and cultural history
* Flora and fauna
* Things to observe on the trail
* Night sky viewing guide
* Fun facts, riddles, and daily challenges
* Scout reflection and journal prompts

The project will produce two versions:

1. **Field Edition** – A lightweight, spiral-bound guide optimized for carrying on the trail.
2. **Collector's Edition** – An expanded full-color reference with additional maps, scientific background, photographs, and historical research.

The goal is to create a guide that transforms a backpacking trek into an immersive educational adventure while serving as a lasting keepsake of the Philmont experience.
